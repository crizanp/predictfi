Protocol Summary

PredictFi is a decentralized YES/NO prediction market protocol on BNB Chain, where users join outcome pools and receive proportional payouts after market resolution.

Winner Calculation (Parimutuel)

For winning side amount W, losing side amount L, user stake s, and platform fee f:

user_payout = s + (s / W) * (L * (1 - f))
