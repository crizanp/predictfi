Prediction Market Whitepaper

Introduction

This whitepaper outlines the design and implementation of a decentralized prediction market platform. The platform allows users to create and participate in markets based on the outcome of future events.

Architecture

The platform is built on a blockchain to ensure transparency and security. It consists of three main components: the smart contract, the user interface, and the oracle system.

Smart Contract

The smart contract manages the creation of markets, the placement of bets, and the distribution of rewards. It ensures that all transactions are executed according to predefined rules.

Oracle System

The oracle system is responsible for providing accurate and timely information about the outcomes of events. It uses a decentralized approach to gather data from multiple sources.

Conclusion

The prediction market platform aims to democratize access to information and provide a transparent mechanism for forecasting future events. By leveraging blockchain technology, it ensures fairness and security for all participants.
